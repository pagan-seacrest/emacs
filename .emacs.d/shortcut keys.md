### EMACS GENERAL DEFINITIONS
- "Frame" is the window
- "Window" is a tab in the "Frame" that may has more then one "Buffer"
- "Buffer" is a single file representation
- "C" means Ctrl
- "M" is "Meta" means Alt
- "S" is Shift
